Marketing

The Problems

Nowadays, more and more people are moving into cities.
As a result, cities will become busier and more compact.
Pollution will worsen as city population grows.
Less people will be farming, so the supply of produce will decrease.
That, in turn, leads to an increase in the demand of produce.
If prices go up, then more people will be in poverty.
Also, most people living in cities are too busy and don't have enough space to garden.

The Solution

Our robot solves every single problem that I wrote about in the paragraph above.
The robot does everything itself, so you don't need to spend much time on the gardening.
In addition, the robot is extremely compact, so it won't take up much space.
For people who care about the environment, plants help reduce carbon dioxide levels through photosynthesis.
