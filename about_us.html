About Us

We are Farm Bot. Farm Bot's mission is to promote local, sustainable agriculture by making farming your own food easy and accessible for everyone in the world, regardless of available space and time commitment, as well as to influence larger scale agricultural operations to be more environmentally friendly through our vertical farming technology. Our Robot can help people to garden for them without having to do any work by appealing to the people who like fresh food without having any land problems. In the next 5 years we plan to expand our retail and having over 1,000 Farm Bots sold all over the USA and also being able to decrease the risk of obesity by having more fresh food for the people.
