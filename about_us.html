About Us

Our goal is to provide individuals with a machine that allows everybody to go along with their daily lives while allowing them to grow their own fresh food. Our Robot can help people to garden for them without having to do any work by appealing to the people who like fresh food without having any land problems. In the next 5 years we plan to expand our retail and having over 1,000 Farm Bots sold all over the USA and also being able to decrease the risk of obesity by having more fresh food for the people.
