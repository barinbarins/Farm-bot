Our Business Plan

Summary and Operations

FarmBot's mission is to promote sustainable agriculture by creating products
that simplifies growing your own food that anyone can use, regardless of time
commitment and available space. Making these products will then generate
funding for R&D in another one of our products, technology in vertical
farming for larger scale agricultural operations.

Organizational Structure

To keep track of all the things that need to be done, the business will be organized into subteams, R&D or Design, Manufacturing, Business, Marketing, and software. Each subteam will have a manager that makes decisions, and will meet with managers of other subteams to ensure that each team's decisions are compatible with each other.
