Our Business Plan

Summary and Operations

FarmBot's mission is to promote sustainable agriculture by creating products
that simplifies growing your own food that anyone can use, regardless of time
commitment and available space. Making these products will then generate
funding for R&D in another one of our products, technology in vertical
farming for larger scale agricultural operations.

Organizational Structure

To keep track of all the things that need to be done, the business will be
organized into subteams, R&D or Design, Manufacturing, Business, Marketing,
and software. Each subteam will have a manager that makes decisions, and will
meet with managers of other subteams to ensure that each team's decisions
are compatible with each other.

Long Term Growth, Stregnths, and Weaknesses

Because of how our product is designed around an autonomous software, the
product is easy to improve and update. We will also be able to capitalize on
the public interest in environmentalism and organic or fresh foods. On the
other hand, the demographic interested and able to purchase the product isn't
high. With our larger scale vertical agricultural technology, it may be too
expesive compared to conventional methods. But it offers them more farmland
without taking much horizontal land, which is appealing to farmers who don't own
as much land. Overtime, we can improve on price and efficiency of our product
to increase consumer demand.
